FAQ

What is the project “The Leaders” about ? + This is the unique collection of 5001 art “Kisses”, by Mindaugas Bonanu. The history of this collection started in 2012, when the first street art “Kiss” was made, which became famous all over the world. All the characters in the new collection are these days Leaders, politicians, the great ones and bad ones, but all of them make decisions that change our lives. Sadly, not all decisions are peaceful, people started to hate each other. So our mission is to reconnect them with Kiss of peace!
What is the benefits of THE LEADERS NFTs ? + Be the part of the community who makes decisions on charity plans in long-term projects and donations.
